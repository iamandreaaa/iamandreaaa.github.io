Hey! I'm Andre'a A., but you can call me Dre'a. I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. Through my hard word, I have been blessed to  generate an incredible connection with those in the media field in the Cleveland scene, but worldwide as well. I'm  truly passionate about helping entrepreneurs turn their creative skills or ideas into profitable brands online.

I consider myself a pretty young and savvy girl. I'm currently  20 years old and just building my little empire from the ground up. I'm currently a freshman in college, studying Business and Media Communication and even with the amazing success with business, I make sure my education comes first and continues to keep my grades up by having a 4.0 GPA. 

In August of 2013, I launched my own jewelry line, Adored Treasures and in a matter of weeks, became an instant success online, of course.  Then, in April of 2013 I took it a step further and  launched my own waist loss line, ADORED TREASURES By Andre'a A. and opened a small shop in Shaker Hts(currently closed).  I have my own Preloved shop, with Vinted. With many projects on the rise for me, let me just say it's just the beginning of what else is to come from this  young and savvy entrepreneur. Over the last couple of years, I  have taken it upon myself  to explore Ohio, as well other states a little deeper whenever I have  the chance to. I offer  weekly content on my blog, Andre'a A.'s Quickguides where I simply  documents how-to's for clients to efficiently run their online business after their current design projects are published. So, I'm very friendly and just want leave you hanging like that. I also have a News section well I should say a Lookbook where I showcases daily outfits, vacations, and even reviews on products from companies I am affifated with.  I'm motivated by my grandparents, and parents  I always pushed myself to be the best me than I  was yesterday, as I continue my strive for greatness.
